Noah Tours Our Story

Noah Tours was established in early 2004 and as you may have guesses, we are named after Noah from the Bible. Noah was the only righteous man of his time and was a kind and gentle soul. Our founders thought it fitting that our company be named after such a man. We aim to be like Noah and always do right by you and all of our travelers. Our team comes from all around the world.
